The Evidence: Where Story Meets Map

The frameworks on this site—from the Glass Box to the 26 Laws—were not created in a vacuum. They were extracted from the wreckage of a 43-year case study detailed in the memoir, From the Storm to the Fire. This page shows the essential link between the theory and the lived terrain.

The Memoir: The Lived Terrain

Part I: The Glass Box

The story of early life trauma, starting at the NICU, establishing the **survival-wired nervous system**. This context is the proof behind the Glass Box Blueprint.

Part II: The Storm

The decades of living with CPTSD adaptations: toxic relationships, emotional dysregulation, and the collapse of identity. This is the **fire** that forged the laws.

Part III: The Map

The process of deconstruction, therapy, and synthesizing the 26 Laws. The **manual** created from the wreckage.
