What This Site Is: Your Map to Healing

This resource is your non-clinical, trauma-informed map built from lived experience and backed by research. We cut through the complicated language to give you clear, actionable frameworks.

Everything here is drawn from the memoir From the Storm to the Fire: 26 Laws of Survival—a 43-year case study detailing what happens when you’re wired for danger from birth and spend decades navigating the aftermath.

Where Should You Start?

If you're here, you're looking for answers. We recommend proceeding in this order:

  1. Understand the Language: Visit the Glossary to translate the complicated clinical terms into plain English.
  2. See the Architecture: Explore the Glass Box Blueprint to understand the foundational wiring of your survival system.
  3. Learn the Laws: Read the 26 Laws—the survival mechanics you developed over time.
  4. Get Context: Review The Evidence to see how the frameworks map directly to the memoir's case study.
Crisis Support: Call or Text 988 (US Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)

The Core Frameworks

The Glass Box Blueprint

This is the origin story. Thirty days in a NICU incubator in 1982—no mother's touch, just machines and alarms. The nervous system learned distance and danger before language existed. This blueprint explains why:

  • The world never felt safe
  • Hypervigilance became baseline
  • Sensory overload was constant
  • Connection felt dangerous

Every framework on this site traces back to this glass box.

Read the Glass Box Blueprint

The 26 Laws of Survival

These laws survived five complete rewrites of the memoir. They're organized into 5 Acts:

  • Act I: Foundations — Early wiring, inherited trauma, dangerous shelter
  • Act II: Battlefields — Work, love, family patterns
  • Act III: Systems & Shadows — Courtrooms, institutions, internal hijackers
  • Act IV: Relapses & Reckonings — Healing spirals, boundaries, addiction
  • Act V: Faith, Memory & Meaning — Integration and carrying the fire

Each law includes a clinical summary and practical takeaway.

Suggested Starting Paths

If You're in Crisis

Skip everything else. Go directly to Crisis Resources. Call or text 988. Safety first. The frameworks can wait.

If You're New to CPTSD

Start with the Glass Box Blueprint to understand where trauma wiring begins. Then read Law #1 and Law #7. Use the Glossary to define unfamiliar terms.

If You're a Survivor Seeking Understanding

Read the 26 Laws in order. Mark the ones that hit. Come back to the Glass Box Blueprint when you're ready to understand the wiring beneath your responses.

If You're a Clinician or Educator

Start with the Glass Box Blueprint for the neurobiological foundation. Review the 26 Laws as teaching points. Explore the Academic Course for deeper curriculum.

If You Love Someone with CPTSD

Start with Law #18: You Can't Trust a Hand on Your Back if You're Still Bracing for a Knife. Then read the Glass Box Blueprint to understand the wiring. Use the Glossary to learn the language.
