The Book & The Site

This site is built on frameworks from the memoir From the Storm to the Fire: 26 Laws of Survival—a 43-year case study of what happens when you're wired for distance and danger from day one. The site provides the map, but the memoir provides the essential context to turn these concepts into survival strategies. Without the lived terrain, the map is just theory.

The Book = The Lived Terrain

Childhood violence, twenty years of toxic marriage, addiction, manipulation, collapse, and the brutal work of understanding it all. The proof that these frameworks work in real life.

The Site = The Clinical Map

The Glass Box Blueprint, the 26 Laws, and every term here started as survival mechanisms before they became teaching tools. The frameworks extracted from the wreckage.

What the Laws Look Like

Law #1

Survival Is Wiring, Not Weakness

Born wired for war, not broken.

When trauma begins at birth, it rewires the nervous system for constant threat detection. What looks like anxiety or overreaction is early wiring doing its job.

Law #26

You Don't Extinguish the Fire—You Learn to Carry It

Live with your fire, don't burn down.

The goal isn't to extinguish the trauma, but to learn to carry it with wisdom. You survived the inferno. Now learn to carry the flame.
