You didn't end up like this by chance. Your nervous system was wired for survival—hypervigilance, emotional dysregulation, relationship chaos, addiction. These aren't character flaws. They're adaptations to impossible circumstances.

This site is the clinical map for understanding Complex PTSD. Clean frameworks. Plain language. No coddling, no bullshit.

The Book and the Map

This site is built on the frameworks from the memoir From the Storm to the Fire: 26 Laws of Survival—a 43-year case study of what happens when you're wired for distance and danger from day one.

The book is the lived terrain. Childhood violence, twenty years of toxic marriage, addiction, manipulation, collapse, and the brutal work of understanding it all.

The site is the clinical map. The Glass Box Blueprint, the 26 Laws, and every term here started as survival mechanisms before they became teaching tools.

You don't need the book to use the site. But the book shows you what these frameworks look like when they collide with real life.
