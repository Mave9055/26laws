What Really Happened

Understanding Complex PTSD Through Lived Experience & Clinical Rigor

The Wounded Blueprint Course

A 16-lecture series designed as a Harvard-style seminar. This curriculum explores the clinical architecture of trauma using From the Storm to the Fire as a primary case study.

PART I: FOUNDATIONS OF TRAUMA

01: The Truth Behind the Silence

Analysis of the initial fracture and the sociopolitical factors of trauma suppression.

02: The Masks We Wear

Understanding adaptive personalities and the survival utility of the false self.

PART II: RELATIONSHIPS & REALITY

03: When Love Hurts

The neurobiology of attachment in high-stress or abusive environments.

04: The War on Reality

Gaslighting, cognitive dissonance, and the systematic dismantling of the victim's agency.

PART III: INTERNAL LANDSCAPE

09: Shame vs. Responsibility

Deconstructing the "Internalized Aggressor" and the burden of unearned guilt.

11: Temporal Dysregulation

Why trauma lives in the "now" and the challenge of establishing a linear future.
