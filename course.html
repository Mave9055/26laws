What Really Happened: Understanding Complex PTSD

A Complete Trauma Education Curriculum

This 16-lecture series uses From the Storm to the Fire as a clinical case study, exploring Complex PTSD through the lens of lived experience and academic rigor.

Harvard-style seminar format with lectures, discussions, role-plays, and practical applications.

Course Structure

Part I: Foundations of Trauma (Lectures 1-2)

  • The Truth Behind the Silence
  • The Masks We Wear

Part II: Relationships & Reality (Lectures 3-4)

  • When Love Hurts
  • The War on Reality

Part III: Coping & Crisis (Lectures 5-8)

  • Relapse Isn't a Betrayal
  • What Healing Actually Looks Like
  • The Breaking Point
  • Rewriting the Legacy

Part IV: Internal Landscape (Lectures 9-12)

  • Shame vs. Responsibility
  • Healing in the Open
  • Temporal Dysregulation
  • From Victim to Agent

Part V: Systemic Change (Lectures 13-16)

  • The Unlearned Lessons
  • Speaking Truth to Power
  • The Crucial Question
  • The Revolution Must Be Trauma-Informed

What's Included

  • 16 complete lecture transcripts (Harvard-style)
  • Discussion prompts and role-play scenarios
  • Study guides and key term glossaries
  • Assignments and reflection exercises
  • Clinical frameworks integrated throughout
